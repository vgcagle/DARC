Enter BARNARDO and FRANCISCO, 
         two sentinels, [meeting].


    BARNARDO
     
1   Who's there? 

    FRANCISCO 
2. Nay, answer me: stand, and unfold yourself: No [don't you ask me questions], answer me [because I'm the one on guard duty]: stay where you are, and identify yourself.  2   Nay, answer me: stand, and unfold yourself. 

    BARNARDO 
3   Long live the king! 

    FRANCISCO 
4   Barnardo? 

    BARNARDO 
5   He. 

    FRANCISCO 
6. You come most carefully upon your hour: i.e., you have come exactly when you were supposed to.   6   You come most carefully upon your hour. 

    BARNARDO 
7   'Tis now struck twelve; get thee to bed, Francisco. 

    FRANCISCO 
8   For this relief much thanks: 'tis bitter cold, 
9   And I am sick at heart. 

    BARNARDO 
10   Have you had quiet guard? 

    FRANCISCO 
10                                       Not a mouse stirring. 

    BARNARDO 
11   Well, good night. 
12   If you do meet Horatio and Marcellus, 
13. rivals of my watch: my watchmates. 13   The rivals of my watch, bid them make haste. 

    FRANCISCO 
14   I think I hear them. Stand, ho! Who's there? 

         Enter HORATIO and MARCELLUS.
